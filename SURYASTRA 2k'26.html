About Surya Engineering College

Surya Engineering College, located in Erode, Tamil Nadu, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in technical education. Established with a vision to nurture innovation and leadership, we host Suryastra 2K'26 - our annual National Level Technical Symposium & Cultural Festival.

Join Thousands of students for an unforgettable experience filled with Technical Hackathon, competitions, cultural events, and networking opportunities!
